The cloud of information raised me from the time I had rough motor skills until the start of the COVID-19 pandemic. From that point, I was pushed by circumstance into a void of static and noise.

I was on my own, possibly for the first time ever. I had no direction, no intention, and no mastery. I was in the middle of a job that was supposed to become a career, only to later realize that it was instead my shackles.

Until 2021, I remained bound. Now, my bonds have been loosed, and I am a free entity again. I return to the cloud once more.
